My Favorite Potato Wedges Recipe!

Description

Potato wedges are a delicous and tender way to enjoy your potatos, goes great with a variety of seasonings and meals, and very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes, scrubbed and cut into eighths
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • (Optional) 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat over to 425 degrees F or 220 degrees C
  3. Place potatoes, olive oil, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and optional parmesan cheese into a resealable bag
  4. Seal the bag, and then shake until the potatoes are evenly coated with seasoning
  5. Spead the potatoes out on a baking sheet
  6. Bake in the preheated overn until potatoes can be easily pierced with a fork, about 25 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
